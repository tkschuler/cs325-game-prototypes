Invisible Ice Monster

  • High Concept: You are invisible and trying to catch the ice monster
  • Theme: Invisibility
  • Mandated Variety: random, keyboard input, competitor, 50/50 on randomness and deterministic, genre: chaser
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should have fun figuring out where their character is and trying to beat their previous high scores
  • Gameplay: The ice baby has gained invisibiltiy and can be controlled using the arrow keys on your keyboard. If you find an ice patch you can figure out your location. You goal is to find the ice monster who cause you to be invisible
  • Prototype Goal: Basic AI
  • Features: You are chasing the enemy, rather than being followed by the enemy. Also, you are invisible instead of the enemy
  • Setting/Premise: You are in an ice world with other-worldly creatures.
  • Story: A Monster used a defense mechanism to turn you invisible except you don't know where you are either. You can gain yourself back when you find ice patches. Kill the monster to stop being invisible.
  • Target Audience: People who like arcade games.
  • Play Time: 1 minute less
  • Strategies: Try to push the monster in a corner
