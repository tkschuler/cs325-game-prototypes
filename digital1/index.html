Space Walk

  • High Concept: Get your player to the opposite corner of the screen to win.
  • Theme: You've been stranded in space. Use your skills to get back to your rocket.
  • Mandated Variety: Ledge jumping game, with villians
  • Player Experience Goals: thrill, skill
  • Gameplay: Player can jump from ledge to ledge trying to make it to the rocket. Player must avoid being hit by a ufo or it is game over. Player must not fall to the bottom of the world or it is game over. The player can jump through platforms from underneath.
  • Prototype Goal: different types of collisions
  • Features: there are ufo's that can destroy you.
  • Setting/Premise: You are an astronaut stranded in space. The only way home is to jump from lunar ledge to lunar ledge back to your spaceship. You have special moon boots that let you hop around in space.
  • Story: This game will have multiple levels and a parallax effect in the future. As the game moves forward, the levels will get harder.
  • Target Audience: People who like arcade games and space.
  • Play Time: 20 seconds
  • Strategies: pay attention to how the ufo's move. they follow a pattern.
