The Great Wizard's Revenge

  • High Concept: Get revenge against the evil warlock who has wrongged you.
  • Theme: Revenge
  • Mandated Variety: random, click + drag.
  • Player Experience Goals: thrill and suspense as they try to kill the warlock.
  • Gameplay: Player must click and drag wizard and get to otherside of the screen to kill evil warlock. The evil warlock has shot out firebolts to try and kill you. Avoid the firebolts including the last 'boss firebolt' to win the game.
  • Prototype Goal: What game mechanic is this prototype evaluating?
  • Features: click and drag instead of cursor keys.
  • Setting/Premise: YOu found the warlock at his volcanic layer.
  • Story: Kill the warlock, he has wrongged you!
  • Target Audience: people who like magic and arcade games.
  • Play Time: 10 seconds
  • Strategies: Notice where the main clump of firebolts is going and run fast away from them
  • Prototype Assets:
    • Warlock
    • Wizard
    • Volcano
  • Prototype Closest Other Game:
    • tween
    • drag overlap function
